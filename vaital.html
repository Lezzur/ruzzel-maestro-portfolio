VaiTAL: AI Medical Test Results Tracker

Progressive web app that transforms scattered lab reports into unified health intelligence. AI extracts biomarkers, tracks trends, flags drug interactions, and provides personalized health insights.

The Challenge

Patients struggle to understand and manage medical test results and medication intake schedules. Critical health information remains siloed across providers, leading to missed drug interactions and suboptimal treatment adherence. Manual tracking is error-prone and time-consuming, creating friction in personalized health management and increasing the risk of adverse outcomes.

The Problem
  • • Scattered lab reports across providers
  • • No trend visibility or context
  • • Medication management is manual & error-prone
  • • Missed drug interactions
  • • Poor treatment adherence

The Solution

An AI-powered platform that ingests medical data from multiple sources, automatically analyzes test results in plain language, tracks medication adherence, flags potential interactions, and provides personalized health insights. Integration with wearables and pharmacy systems creates a unified health intelligence layer, resulting in improved medication adherence and reduced emergency interventions.

Key Capabilities
  • • AI-powered biomarker extraction
  • • Trend analysis & visualization
  • • Drug interaction detection
  • • Medication adherence tracking
  • • Wearable & pharmacy integration
