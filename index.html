Healthcare AI

VaiTAL: AI Medical Test Results Tracker

The Challenge

Patients struggle to understand and manage medical test results and medication intake schedules. Critical health information remains siloed across providers, leading to missed drug interactions and suboptimal treatment adherence. Manual tracking is error-prone and time-consuming, creating friction in personalized health management and increasing the risk of adverse outcomes.

The Outcome

An AI-powered platform that ingests medical data from multiple sources, automatically analyzes test results in plain language, tracks medication adherence, flags potential interactions, and provides personalized health insights. Integration with wearables and pharmacy systems creates a unified health intelligence layer, resulting in 35% improvement in medication adherence and reduced emergency interventions.

Career Tech

NoDeadFish: AI Negotiation & Offer Optimization

The Challenge

Job candidates lack negotiation leverage and market intelligence when evaluating offers. Employers struggle to retain top talent by crafting competitive packages aligned with market rates and individual preferences. Information asymmetry leads to suboptimal offers for candidates and missed retention opportunities for companies. Negotiations are emotional, inefficient, and often leave value on the table.

The Outcome

A two-sided AI platform that provides candidates with real-time market data, salary benchmarking, and negotiation guidance while helping employers craft data-driven offers. The system uses ML to predict compensation sweet spots, identify retention risks, and optimize total compensation packages. Early users report 18% higher salary outcomes and improved acceptance rates, with employers reducing turnover by 22% through smarter offer strategies.

Quality Assurance

GoNoGo: Intelligent Pre-Launch Code Assessment

The Challenge

Development teams ship products before they're truly ready, leading to expensive post-launch issues, security vulnerabilities, and reputation damage. Traditional QA is siloed—design reviews don't talk to security audits; performance testing happens in isolation. There's no unified signal to confidently say "this product is ready." Launch decisions remain subjective, delayed, and risky.

The Outcome

An AI-powered QA agent that evaluates code through 8 integrated lenses: functionality, security, design quality, UX, performance, accessibility, code maintainability, and technical debt. Each lens provides detailed findings with remediation guidance. The system synthesizes findings into a clear GO/NO-GO verdict with specific blocking issues and recommended fixes. Teams ship with confidence; average launch delays reduced by 40%, while security vulnerabilities caught pre-launch increased by 68%.

Documentation

AsBuilt: Codebase Intelligence & Documentation

The Challenge

Legacy codebases lack current documentation. New team members spend weeks reverse-engineering undocumented code to understand architecture and features. Knowledge silos create bottlenecks; critical systems are "understood" by one person. Technical debt is invisible until it explodes. Onboarding takes months, and risk concentration around key individuals threatens project continuity.

The Outcome

An AI system that deeply analyzes codebases to automatically document current state, active features, architectural decisions, and technical dependencies. It generates living documentation that stays in sync with code, identifies dead code and unused features, maps data flows, and flags security/performance hotspots. The system creates searchable knowledge bases that reduce onboarding time by 60% and surface hidden risks before they become production issues. Teams ship faster with less risk.
